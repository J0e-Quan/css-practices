A day in the Plushie Kingdom

Sharing all my experiences during a day-trip to the Plushie Kingdom.

How to get there

Located in Penang, the Plushie Kingdom is quite accessible. You can get there by using:

  • Ferry
  • Car
  • Bus
  • Airplane

Getting in

To get in, go through biosecurity before entering the Kingdom. Here's how it works:

  1. Reach the Sofa
  2. Get to know the people working at the Ministry of Immigration
  3. Follow the procedures...
  4. ...and you're done!

It can get quite busy at times, but there's quite a lot of information available on their website!

What its like to be there

Life in the Kingdom is very relaxed. It's home to plushies from all over the world, so everyone's super welcoming and inclusive! I had a great time there, exploring the Kingdom and trying out all the food there.

I had a great time at the Plushie Kingdom! A second visit's definetly on my to-do list!
